Hey there! My name is Bill Guo and I’m currently a Computer Engineering student
at University of Waterloo.

I absolutely love advanced technology and computers. I believe that in the near future computers will change the way people live. Even more than they have already. I would really love that one day my creations/works are used by everyone in the future.That’s why I choose to study computer in the university.

Although I started to learn coding at University, I learned many programing languages and algorithms during my spare time. Meanwhile, I did many different coding projects to practise the knowledge I learned because practice make it better.

My first intership was QA testing position at Vocera where I learned how to test a program and how agile development works. The second inter-job I had was Application programmer at Ontario Ministry of Education where i learnt JavaScript to debug and program.

My aspiration is to be part of a team that engineers great products that are used by people everyday just like the iPhone.

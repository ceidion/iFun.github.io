Hey there! My name is Bill Guo and I’m currently a Computer Engineering student at University of Waterloo.

I absolutely love advanced technology and computers. I believe in the near future computers will change the way people live. I would really love to see that my projects/work is used by everyone in the future. That’s why I choose to study computer in the university.

Although I started to learn coding at University, I learned many programing languages and algorithms on my spare time. Meanwhile, I did many different coding projects to practices the knowledge I learned because practice makes better.

My first inter-job is QA testing at Vocera where I learned how to test a program and how agile development works. My second inter-job I had is Application programmer at Ontario Ministry of Education where I had to learn JavaScript to debug and program.

I am considering myself as a computer geek. I like to collect computer gears and follow the news of technology. I have an unprinted black mechanical keyboard that my friends do not understand why I like it unprinted (Because it looks awesome).

My aspiration is to be part of a team that engineers great products and used by people everyday just like the iPhone.
